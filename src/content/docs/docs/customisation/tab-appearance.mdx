Customise how Bonjourr appears in your browser tabs by changing its title and favicon. The title can contain up to 80 characters, and the favicon can be changed using emojis.

<SmallDocsImage>
  ![Bonjourr's tab appearance settings](@assets/documentation/customisation/tab-appearance.png)
</SmallDocsImage>

## Emoji support

Emoji favicons are supported differently depending on your browser:

- Firefox supports them, but scaling and alignment may not always be perfect ([see issue](https://github.com/victrme/Bonjourr/issues/431)).
- Safari currently does not support emoji favicons.

### Adding emojis

You can enter emojis using your operating system's emoji picker:

- Windows: <kbd>WIN</kbd> + <kbd>.</kbd>
- macOS: <kbd>Ctrl</kbd> + <kbd>Cmd</kbd> + <kbd>Space</kbd>

You can also copy and paste emojis from the [Unicode emoji list](https://unicode.org/emoji/charts/full-emoji-list.html).
