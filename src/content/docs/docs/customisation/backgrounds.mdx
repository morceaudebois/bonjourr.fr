Bonjourr lets you personalise your new tab with dynamic backgrounds. Choose from curated image and video collections, your own files, remote URLs, solid colors, and more.

![A minimal Bonjourr config with a clock and a clean background](@assets/documentation/customisation/backgrounds.png)

## Images

Use your own image sources or let Bonjourr provide curated photography through Unsplash.

### Bonjourr Daylight

By default, Bonjourr uses the [Unsplash API](https://unsplash.com/developers) to fetch its backgrounds from a feature we call Bonjourr Daylight. Images are manually curated into four collections that change according to the time of the day:

- One for [the morning](<https://unsplash.com/collections/GD4aOSg4yQE/bonjourr-backgrounds-(noon)>) (2h around sunrise).
- One for [the day](<https://unsplash.com/collections/o8uX55RbBPs/bonjourr-backgrounds-(day)>).
- One for [the evening](<https://unsplash.com/collections/3M2rKTckZaQ/bonjourr-backgrounds-(evening)>) (2h around sunset).
- And one for [the night](<https://unsplash.com/collections/bHDh4Ae7O8o/bonjourr-backgrounds-(night)>).

<Aside type="tip">Right-click on the page to skip to the next background.</Aside>

### Unsplash Collections

You can use any collection on Unsplash as a background source in Bonjourr. This allows you to carefully curate your own images from Unsplash.

<LazyVideo src="/documentation/customisation/custom-unsplash-collection.mp4" autoplay={false} />

<Steps>
1. #### Find an Unsplash collection

   Go to <a href="https://unsplash.com/">**unsplash.com**</a>, search for a topic you're interested in, then open the **Collections** tab and choose a collection you like.

2. #### Copy the collection ID

   The collection ID is part of the URL. For example, if the URL is:

   `https://unsplash.com/collections/2170139/wolfdogs-of-unsplash`,

   then the collection ID is: `2170139`

3. #### Paste the ID into Bonjourr

   In Bonjourr, enable **Show all settings** at the top of the settings panel. Paste the collection ID into the **Unsplash collection** field. You can use multiple collections by separating their IDs with commas.

</Steps>

### Unsplash Search

If you have a theme in mind, you can use the Unsplash Search feature and let Unsplash do the work for you: just type in a keyword and it will start fetching backgrounds related to that keyword. You can even set several of them by separating them with a comma.


### URLs

The URLs option is another way to import your own images into Bonjourr, but instead of having them on your computer, you source them from a remote server.

<SmallDocsImage>
	![Bonjourr's background URLs option](@assets/documentation/customisation/background-urls.png)
</SmallDocsImage>

This is great if you want **more flexibility**. If you're a power user, you could do something like setting up a dynamic URL where the backgrounds change according to your exact preferences, such as certain times of the day or month.

The Background URLs option has another advantage over Local files: it can be synced across browsers.

## Videos

Thanks to [Pixabay](https://pixabay.com/)'s free video library, Bonjourr lets you set videos as your backgrounds. Video backgrounds add motion to your new tab, but they require more processing power than images and may increase battery usage.

Additionally, we try to go out of our way to never integrate AI generated content to our background collections. If anything AI were to slip through, be sure to tell us about it [via email](mailto:bonjourr.app@protonmail.com) and we'll gladly remove it.

### Bonjourr Daylight

Just like [with images](/docs/customisation/backgrounds/#bonjourr-daylight), Bonjourr also offers curated video backgrounds that change with the time of day.

### Pixabay Search

Similar to [Unsplash Search](#unsplash-search), you can enter keywords in Bonjourr to fetch related videos from Pixabay's library.

## Local files (images & videos)

The local files option allows you to easily set images or videos from your device to Bonjourr.

Click <kbd>+ Add new</kbd> to import files from your computer. Most video and image file formats are supported, and Bonjourr will handle duplicates if need be.


<SmallDocsImage>
	![Bonjourr's local files section](@assets/documentation/customisation/local-files.png)
</SmallDocsImage>

By selecting an image or video and clicking <bjrbtn>Options</bjrbtn>, you can individually adjust the positioning, size and playback speed of each file. Reducing the zoom to its minimum sets the size automatically.

<Aside type="caution">
	**Local files are not synced between devices or included in the export file.** If you need syncing, use the <a href="#urls">URLs</a> option instead.
</Aside>


## Solid color

The solid color option allows you to choose a specific color for your background if you like to keep things especially minimalist.

## Texture overlay

Textures are decorative overlays applied on top of any background. There are many types you can try, and they will typically look better over a blurred background or a solid color.

![Showing off what you can do with Bonjourr's textures](@assets/documentation/customisation/textures.png)

Most textures can be edited through their color, opacity and density to get to the perfect result.

## Fade in time

Control how long the background takes to fade in when opening a new tab.

Setting the slider to the minimum disables the transition. This may cause a brief flicker while image and video backgrounds load. 

<Aside type="note">Since they don't need to load, solid color backgrounds are always displayed instantly.</Aside>
