## General usage

Right-click the clock and date widget to open its settings. From there, you can adjust time zones, date format, size, and other display options.

## Regular (digital) clock

The default digital clock includes standard configuration options such as 12/24-hour format, seconds display, and AM/PM indicators.

## Analog clock

If you're feeling fancy, Bonjourr integrates an analog clock option that is quite customisable. Shape, face, hands, background, border... There is much you can change to create your own perfect looking clock.

<img src="/documentation/overview/clock-styles.png" alt="Bonjourr's clock styles" loading="lazy" />

Some good things to know during customisation:

- Clicking the 🌙/☀️ icons on the sliders will switch from black to white.
- The analog clock background is blurred when its opacity is above 0.
- Enabling seconds on the analog clock is generally advised.

## World clocks

<img
  src="/documentation/overview/world-clocks.png"
  alt="Bonjourr's world clock feature"
  loading="lazy"
/>

**World clocks** allows you to add multiple clocks with different time zones. Removing the name of a world clock will remove the clock entirely.
