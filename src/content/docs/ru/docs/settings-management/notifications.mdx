## Review or donate notification

This notification encourages you to donate to the project or leave a review.

![The review notification in action](/documentation/settings-management/review-notification.jpg)

It usually shows up after you've been using Bonjourr for a few days, and in the recent updates, goes away automatically after a while if you don't interact with it.

<Aside type="tip" icon="question-circle" title="Sure but how do I get rid of it NOW?">
	Remove it forever by clicking anywhere on the notification.
</Aside>

## Update notification

The rarest type of notifications in Bonjourr! They'll usually inform you of our brand new major update and link to its corresponding blog post. Do check it out, it's human-written 👀

![The update notification in action](/documentation/settings-management/update-notification.jpg)

You can disable these notifications using the **Updates** toggle in the Notifications section of the settings (visible after having toggled **Show all settings**).

## ❤️ Supporters notification

The supporters notification was introduced with Bonjourr 20.4 and is our way to thank people who support us [on Ko-Fi](https://ko-fi.com/bonjourr). It appears at the start of each month and reflects the donations of the previous month.

![The supporters notification in action](/documentation/overview/supporters.png)

If you'd like your name to appear in the supporters notification, you can [donate on Ko-Fi](https://ko-fi.com/bonjourr). If you'd like to donate without having your name appear in it, be sure to send us a message when donating.

The supporters notification can be fully disabled in the settings panel, **Show all settings** first, then **Supporters notification** in the **Notifications** section.
