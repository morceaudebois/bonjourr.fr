Here comes our new Bonjourr update! This one has been in the works for a long time and brings something you've been asking for even longer: background flexibility! Let's dive in. :)

🎥 Video backgrounds are here!

Thanks to Pixabay's free video library and their community, you will now be able to bring your homepage to life by setting videos as backgrounds in Bonjourr.

Using a fancy video as a background

It works the same way as it's always worked with the regular backgrounds: either use our curated selection of videos that change according to the time of the day (renamed Bonjourr Daylight), or set custom terms that will fetch related videos.

🌫️ Texture overlays to spice things up

The texture overlay is a brand new option you can add to any type of background, be it photo, video or solid color. You can choose from a list of over 20 different textures that go over your regular backgrounds, such as grain, dots, lines and plenty of others. Each texture can be customized: change their opacity, their density and even their color at your heart's will.

Some of the things you can try with texture overlays

This adds a huge layer a customization to make Bonjourr feel even more yours. We can't wait to see what you guys come up with!

🟥 Solid color backgrounds for the minimalists

If you tend to enjoy a more minimalist style, you might be interested in our new solid color background feature which lets you choose a specific color directly from Bonjourr's UI.

Solid color backgrounds allow for a more focused experience

This can be nice if you find photos or videos to be distracting and want to focus on the practicality and your productivity. Solid color backgrounds also tend to go really well with overlay textures.

🔎 Background search and improved local files management

You might be used to the custom Unsplash collection feature to set specific images from Unsplash. This works well if you're a power user, but it can feel a little cumbersome. Fortunately, Bonjourr 21 allows you to set custom image themes right from the UI: just select Unsplash Search or Pixabay Search as your provider and enter the keywords you're interested in.

Setting a theme is easier than ever

We also took a lot of time to improve the local files selector. It now allows you to choose how each image should be displayed as a background: you can zoom in and move its position vertically and horizontally. Some quality of life features have been added you'll enjoy especially if you've got lots of images: using ctrl/cmd + click, you can select multiple images and delete them all at once. It will even filter out duplicates before uploading.

Our new local files options

Lastly, we're introducing a new option enabled by default that compresses the larger images you might upload. This will help with overall performance and should not have a noticeable impact on the quality of your images.

✨ Other miscellaneous improvements

We're not done! Three more important things to note:

    You can now control the backgrounds (download, pause and skip) right from the homepage. Simply hover at the bottom and new buttons will appear.
    If you like things fast, Bonjourr 21 allows you to control the fade in speed of the backgrounds from the settings.
    Thanks to @Stelage's work, you can now choose quotes from The Office!

Get quotes from The Office

Lots of bugs and general improvements have been made, including:

    Improved ultrawide monitor support by limiting the cropping of images to 2/1
    Way better performance when background blur is enabled on images (they get scaled down when blur is enabled)
    Fixed the search bar leading to broken sites when using dots in the query
    Fixed weather section appearing while disabled in some cases
    Fixed timezone issue (see GitHub for details)
    Fixed search bar issue with Chinese websites

Bonjourr 21 makes it easier to download, pause and skip images
